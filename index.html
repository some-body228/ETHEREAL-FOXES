THE STORY

There is a world of Cryptocurrency. There are many countries, continents, villages and even some uncharted lands in this world. But we are going to talk about one of the most powerful continents in the world. It is the continent of Ethereum. This continent is inhabited by the Ethereal foxes; they are all of different colours and all of them have different hobbies and different positions in society. What is so special about them? You can see an Ethereum above the head of each of them; everyone has a different one, some have the entirety of it, others miss a part of the thing, a few one miss just a tiny bit of it.  

The currency on the continent is so-called «Fame». It is a virtual coin that can someone can be awarded with for completing certain tasks. But not everyone can take any of the tasks. The harder you want your task to be, the more educated you should be. For harder tasks you obviously get more money. These hard tasks are about sales, gaming industry, modeling etc. You should have a certain education to solve them. The testing was organized every year for every citizen of the continent, and the results of this test was defining the Difficulty ant the Area of tasks. The test combined all the areas of subjects. It was the most important and hard day for everyone.  

The results were very good in the big cities, there were many foxes, who could take the most difficult tasks, for which they were paid for months in advance. But with the prosperity of big cities, the small towns came to decline. It was so because the tasks were rarely reaching the settlements, since the residents of these villages had a low level of education; payment for tasks was barely enough for a week. The inequality was the reason why the crime level in the villages has increased.  

In the beginning, the foxes did not pay attention to this, because they did not see a difference between the kinds of ethereum everyone had. But when they saw him, the only one who was so ruthless that he broke his Ether completely, and they saw how disfigured he became, how his fur became almost colorless, how his eyes turned white and how even in his head something has changed. Foxes like him started to be called Hollows. At first, no one could understand why this happened to him. But when there was more news about Hollows, everyone began to realize.  

Those interested in this topic have found a pattern. First, each of the Hollows committed some kind of crime (There was information about this in newspapers, when the criminal was caught). Second, many of them lived below the poverty line, in dangerous areas. And most importantly, the third. Each of them had very little Ethereum left. Everything worked out in the heads of the Foxes, they realized that, having completely lost Ethereum, you would lose yourself.  

Massive paranoia has begun because of this, many foxes were afraid to do anything, because they knew that after a complete breakdown of Ethereum, they could cease to exist, no one wanted such a fate for themselves. To stop the riots, a special profession, Ethereum Blacksmith, was created. Since the continent was huge, and there were five large cities on the continent (not counting many small villages and camps), it was decided to hire five blacksmiths. Blacksmith of the Forest City, Blacksmith of the Sea City, Blacksmith of the Desert City, Blacksmith of the Mountain City and Blacksmith of the Central City.  

Five foxes were chosen; those whose Ethers were still inviolable. Blacksmiths were given a certain power and strength. Not only did they see the Ethereums of others; they could also create a jewelry that shows the state of your Ethereum in real time. Also, blacksmiths were endowed with immortality, calmness and prudence, which helped the Blacksmiths not to commit crimes. Also, Ethereun of the Blacksmith looked a little different than the others’, it was painted in the colors of their hometown. But the services of the blacksmiths weren't that cheap. Not everyone could afford a pendant or earring with their Ethereum. And some citizens simply did not want to know what quality their Ethereum was, because they were living an ordinary life.  

But sure their lives weren't all about robberies, no. These were exceptional cases when the crop did not sprout, when there was a drought, when one of his relatives fell seriously ill. These situations were rare, but it does not negate the need for theft.
